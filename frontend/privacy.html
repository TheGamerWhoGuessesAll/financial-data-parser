Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At FinParse Pro, we prioritize data minimization. We only collect the information necessary to provide you with our parsing services.

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and name.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous telemetry regarding how our web application is accessed and used.

2. How We Handle Your Financial Data (Zero Retention)

We do not store, view, or sell any of the financial files (e.g., CSVs, spreadsheets) you upload. All expense parsing is performed securely in real-time. Once the parsing session is complete and you close your browser, your uploaded data is permanently cleared from our memory servers.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party payment processors (such as Stripe) and authentication providers (such as Google OAuth). We do not store your credit card information directly on our servers.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at privacy@finparse.dev.
